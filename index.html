HOW TO PLAY

Drain your opponent's health, then pin them for a 3-count or lock in a submission. Or knock them off the platform and win by a 10-count ring-out.

Keyboard

  • W A S D — move around the ring
  • J — quick strike (chain 3 for a combo)
  • K — heavy strike (stuns, knocks down)
  • L — grapple into a clinch
  • SPACE — pin a downed foe / mash to break free
  • E — climb into the ring or onto the apron
  • T — taunt to recover stamina
  • P / ESC — pause

In a clinch

  • J — suplex
  • K — bodyslam (or throw out near the ropes)
  • L — submission hold
  • Grabbed? Mash SPACE to break free, or L to reverse.
  • Being pinned? Mash SPACE / J to kick out.
  • In a hold? Mash SPACE / J to escape before you tap.

Touch devices: use the on-screen stick and buttons (J, K, L, SPC, E).
